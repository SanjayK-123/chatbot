Museums are dynamic institutions that collect, conserve, and exhibit items of artistic, historical, or scientific significance. They act as gateways to the past, present,and future by providing immersive experiences and educational opportunities. Through thoughtfully designed exhibitions and interactive installations, museums invite visitors to explore and connect with different cultures and ideas.Museums collect and preserve our objects and materials of religious, cultural and historical value. They are a good source of entertainment. These museums help to preserve and promote our cultural heritage.
